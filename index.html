What Mati does

What Mati does.

Mati takes the key details of your project and builds the commercial case from them.

Revenue is calculated from what the space can realistically do — based on location, concept, size, and how similar operations perform.

Costs, payroll, and rent are measured against real benchmarks.

The result is a clear and rational view of what the site can deliver.

Why this matters

Why this matters.

Every project carries two realities at once: the business must operate, and the capital must return.

Mati evaluates both within the same structure and brings them together at the decision point.

The benchmarks are based on observed market behaviour and experience.

You get a position you can defend — before any commitment is made.

The process

Five steps. One structured decision.

1

You provide the inputs.

Location, concept, rent, size, and investment.

2

Mati builds the case.

Revenue, costs, payroll, and break-even are calculated from your inputs and calibrated against market benchmarks.

3

The case is tested.

The project is assessed against operating limits, cost pressures, and weaker performance scenarios. Where the risks are, they are identified.

4

The project is assessed from two sides.

Operational viability and investment return.

5

The outcome is direct:

Proceed. Adjust the terms. Do not pursue — with the reasoning visible and open to challenge.
